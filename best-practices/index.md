## Quick Tips

Before diving into the detailed guides, here are the most impactful things you can do today:

1. **Upload your FAQ as a knowledge base source** — This single action dramatically improves reply quality
2. **Set your brand voice with specific examples** — "Be friendly" is vague; "Use short sentences, address by name, mention free shipping" is actionable
3. **Start moderation at medium sensitivity** — Adjust up or down based on actual results
4. **Check your dashboard daily for the first week** — Early monitoring lets you fine-tune before bad habits form
5. **Respond to purchase-intent comments fastest** — These are your highest-value interactions
