# ROAS Improvement Playbook

Businesses using [Rypl](https://www.rypl.is) typically see approximately **10% improvement in ROAS** within their first month. This playbook breaks down exactly how moderation, AI replies, and intent detection work together to drive higher return on ad spend — and gives you a week-by-week plan to implement it.

---

## How Rypl Improves ROAS

ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) improves when you get more revenue from the same ad budget. Rypl improves ROAS through three mechanisms:

### 1. Moderation Protects Your Ad Performance

Spam and negative comments reduce ad effectiveness:

- **Social proof damage** — Spam-filled comment sections make your brand look neglected
- **Algorithm impact** — Facebook's algorithm considers engagement quality; spam engagement is low quality
- **Buyer hesitation** — Potential buyers read comments before purchasing. Negative or spam-heavy comments increase drop-off

By hiding spam in ~1.7 seconds and handling negative comments constructively, Rypl keeps your ad comments clean, credible, and conversion-friendly.

### 2. AI Replies Convert Commenters into Buyers

Every comment is a potential customer reaching out. Without Rypl, most comments go unanswered — and the buyer moves on. With Rypl:

- **28% more conversations are converted into sales**
- Replies arrive in under 60 seconds — while the customer is still engaged
- Context-aware replies address the specific question with accurate product information
- Purchase-intent comments get immediate, action-oriented responses with links to buy

### 3. Intent Detection Optimizes Your Strategy

Understanding what your audience wants helps you spend smarter:

- **High Purchase Intent ads** deserve more budget — they attract buyers
- **High Support/Complaint ads** may need creative updates to set better expectations
- **High Price Inquiry ads** suggest interest but price friction — test promotions or payment plans
- **Intent trends over time** reveal which products and offers resonate most

## Week-by-Week Implementation Plan

### Week 1: Foundation

**Goal: Get Rypl running and collecting baseline data.**

- [ ] Create your Rypl account and connect your Facebook Pages
- [ ] Upload your product catalog and FAQ as knowledge base
- [ ] Set your brand voice with at least 5 example replies
- [ ] Configure moderation at medium sensitivity
- [ ] Activate your AI agent
- [ ] Note your current ROAS baseline for comparison

**Expected results:** Spam reduction, first AI replies posted, initial dashboard data.

### Week 2: Optimize Replies

**Goal: Improve reply quality based on first week data.**

- [ ] Review 50+ AI replies from Week 1
- [ ] Identify knowledge gaps (questions the AI could not answer well)
- [ ] Add missing information to your knowledge base
- [ ] Refine brand voice based on how replies read in context
- [ ] Add specific reply guidance for your most common comment types
- [ ] Check moderation logs for false positives and adjust

**Expected results:** Noticeably better reply quality, fewer generic responses.

### Week 3: Leverage Intent Data

**Goal: Use intent analytics to inform ad strategy.** (Pro and Ultra plans)

- [ ] Review intent distribution across your ad campaigns
- [ ] Identify your highest-performing ads (most Purchase and Price Inquiry intents)
- [ ] Allocate more budget to high-intent ads
- [ ] Reduce budget or update creative on ads with high Complaint intents
- [ ] Add pricing and promotional information to knowledge base for Price Inquiry responses
- [ ] Ensure purchase-intent replies include direct buy links

**Expected results:** Better budget allocation, more purchase-ready traffic receiving immediate replies.

### Week 4: Measure and Scale

**Goal: Quantify ROAS improvement and plan next steps.**

- [ ] Compare current ROAS to your Week 1 baseline
- [ ] Review conversion metrics: comments to sales, click-through rates on reply links
- [ ] Document what worked and what needs more refinement
- [ ] Consider adding more knowledge base sources for continued improvement
- [ ] Evaluate whether to upgrade your plan for additional features
- [ ] Set ongoing weekly review cadence (15-30 minutes)

**Expected results:** Measurable ROAS improvement, clear roadmap for continued optimization.

## The Three Pillars Working Together

The real power of Rypl comes from the three pillars working in combination:

```
Clean Comments (Moderation)
    + Relevant Replies (AI + Knowledge Base)
    + Smart Targeting (Intent Detection)
    = Higher ROAS
```

**Without moderation:** Great replies get buried in spam, and potential buyers leave.

**Without good replies:** Clean comment sections are nice, but unanswered questions mean lost sales.

**Without intent data:** You are optimizing blind — spending the same on high-intent and low-intent ads.

All three together create a flywheel: cleaner comments attract more genuine engagement, better replies convert more of that engagement into sales, and intent data helps you invest in the campaigns that drive the most revenue.

## Ongoing Optimization

ROAS improvement is not a one-time event. Continue to optimize:

- **Monthly:** Refresh knowledge base with new products, updated pricing, seasonal offers
