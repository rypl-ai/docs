# Getting Started with Rypl

Welcome to [Rypl](https://www.rypl.is) — the AI-powered platform that automates your Facebook ad comment management. This guide walks you through every step from creating your account to having a fully operational AI agent replying to your ad comments.

The entire setup process takes approximately 10-15 minutes. Most users see their first automated reply within an hour of going live.

---

## Setup Overview

Setting up Rypl involves four straightforward steps:

### Step 1: [Create Your Account](sign-up.md)

Sign up at [app.rypl.is](https://app.rypl.is/signup) and start your 7-day free trial. Every plan includes full access to all features during the trial period so you can evaluate Rypl with zero risk.

### Step 2: [Connect Your Facebook Page](connect-facebook.md)

Link your Facebook Page through Meta's Official Graph API. This secure OAuth flow ensures Rypl has the permissions it needs to read comments and post replies on your behalf — with zero ban risk.

### Step 3: [Create an AI Agent](create-ai-agent.md)

Configure your AI agent by setting a brand voice, uploading your knowledge base (product catalog, FAQs, website content), and defining moderation rules. Each agent can be tailored to a specific Facebook Page or client.

### Step 4: [Go Live](go-live.md)

Activate your agent and let Rypl take over. In your first 24 hours, you will see spam hidden in ~1.7 seconds, AI replies sent in under 60 seconds, and real-time analytics tracking every interaction.

---

## What You Will Need

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- **A Facebook account** with admin access to the Facebook Page(s) you want to manage
- **An active Facebook ad campaign** with comments enabled (or upcoming campaigns)
- **Product/service information** to upload as a knowledge base (PDFs, CSVs, or website URLs work great)
- **An email address** for your Rypl account

---

## What Happens After Setup

Once your agent is live, Rypl works around the clock:

- **Spam and negative comments** are detected and hidden in approximately 1.7 seconds
- **Genuine customer questions** receive context-aware AI replies in under 60 seconds
- **Every comment is classified** by intent (Purchase, Price Inquiry, Support, etc.) for tracking
- **Your dashboard** updates in real-time with engagement metrics, response rates, and conversion data

On average, businesses see a 10% improvement in ROAS within their first month and 28% more conversations converted into sales.
