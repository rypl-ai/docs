# Go Live

You have created your account, connected your Facebook Page, and configured your AI agent. Now it is time to activate Rypl and let it start working for you.

---

## Activating Your Agent

From the Rypl dashboard:

1. Navigate to your AI agent's settings page
2. Confirm that the agent is assigned to the correct Facebook Page(s)
3. Toggle the agent status to **Active**
4. Rypl immediately begins monitoring your ad comments

That is it. Your agent is now live and working around the clock.

---

## What to Expect in the First 24 Hours

### Immediate (0-5 minutes)

- Rypl starts scanning incoming comments on your connected Pages
- New spam comments are detected and hidden within approximately **1.7 seconds**
- The agent begins analyzing comments for intent and context

### First Hour

- Genuine customer comments receive AI-generated replies in **under 60 seconds**
- Your dashboard starts populating with real-time metrics: comments processed, replies sent, spam hidden
- Intent classification tags (Purchase, Price Inquiry, Support, etc.) appear on each comment

### First 24 Hours

- You will have a clear picture of your comment volume and engagement patterns
- Response rates, reply quality, and moderation accuracy are visible in the dashboard
- The AI learns from your knowledge base and refines its responses based on the context of each conversation

### First Week

- Engagement metrics stabilize, giving you reliable baseline data
- You can start comparing pre-Rypl and post-Rypl performance
- Fine-tune your agent based on real-world performance (adjust brand voice, expand knowledge base, tweak moderation rules)

### First Month

- Businesses typically see approximately **10% improvement in ROAS**
- **28% more conversations** are converted into sales
- You have enough data to optimize your agent configuration for maximum impact

## Monitoring Your Agent

### Dashboard Metrics

Keep an eye on these key metrics after going live:

- **Comments Processed** — Total number of comments your agent has handled
- **Replies Sent** — How many AI replies were generated and posted
- **Spam Hidden** — Number of spam comments automatically hidden
- **Average Reply Time** — Should be under 60 seconds
- **Intent Breakdown** — Distribution of comment intents across your ads
- **Engagement Rate** — How commenters respond to AI replies

### Review AI Replies

In the first few days, we recommend spot-checking your agent's replies to ensure they match your brand voice and provide accurate information. Look for:

- **Accuracy** — Are product details, prices, and policies correct?
- **Tone** — Does the reply match your desired brand voice?
- **Completeness** — Are customer questions fully answered?
- **Relevance** — Is the reply appropriate for the specific comment?

If you notice issues, update your [brand voice](../configuration/brand-voice.md) or [knowledge base](../configuration/knowledge-base.md) configuration accordingly.

---

## Fine-Tuning After Launch

### Common Adjustments

- **Knowledge base gaps** — If the agent cannot answer certain questions, add relevant information to your knowledge base
- **Brand voice tweaks** — Adjust tone or add style guidelines based on how replies read in context
- **Moderation sensitivity** — Tighten or loosen rules based on false positives (legitimate comments hidden) or false negatives (spam getting through)

### When to Intervene Manually

Rypl handles the vast majority of comments automatically, but there are situations where manual intervention is appropriate:

- **Complex customer complaints** that require personal attention
- **Legal or compliance-sensitive** comments
- **Opportunities for direct sales conversations** that benefit from a human touch
- **Unusual situations** the AI has not been trained to handle
