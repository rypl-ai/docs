## GPT-4 mini (Simple Plan)

**Available on:** Simple plan ($19/mo, $15/mo yearly)

GPT-4 mini is a fast, efficient model well-suited for straightforward comment management. It handles the most common types of Facebook ad comments effectively:

### Strengths

- **Speed:** Fast response generation for high-volume comment sections
- **Cost efficiency:** Powers the most affordable Rypl plan
- **Basic moderation:** Effective spam detection and negative comment filtering
- **Auto-replies:** Generates relevant replies for common comment types
- **Multi-language:** Supports all languages for detection and reply

### Best For

- Small businesses with a single Facebook Page
- Moderate comment volumes
- Straightforward products with simple Q&A patterns
- Businesses getting started with AI comment management

### Limitations

- Does not support knowledge base integration — replies are based on general understanding
- No intent detection — comments are not classified by category
- Limited context awareness — replies may be more generic
- Basic conversational ability — does not maintain conversation history

---

## GPT-4.1 (Pro Plan)

**Available on:** Pro plan ($49/mo, $39/mo yearly)

GPT-4.1 is a significant step up in capability. It unlocks Rypl's most powerful features including knowledge base integration, intent detection, and context-aware replies.

### Strengths

- **Context-aware replies:** Understands the ad context, previous comments, and conversation flow
- **Knowledge base integration:** Pulls specific product details, pricing, and policies from your uploaded sources
- **Intent detection:** Classifies every comment into one of 8 intent categories
- **Post content analysis:** Reads your actual ad creative and copy for additional context
- **Advanced moderation:** More nuanced understanding of what constitutes spam vs. legitimate criticism
- **Better tone control:** More accurately matches your configured brand voice

### Best For

- Growing businesses running multiple ad campaigns
- E-commerce stores with extensive product catalogs
- Businesses that need accurate, detailed replies about specific products
- Agencies managing multiple clients (up to 5 accounts, 20 pages)
- Campaigns where reply quality directly impacts conversion rates

### Key Advantage Over GPT-4 mini

The biggest difference is knowledge base integration. With GPT-4.1, your agent can answer "How much is the blue one in size large?" with the exact price and a link to that specific variant. GPT-4 mini would give a more generic response.

## GPT-5 (Ultra Plan)

**Available on:** Ultra plan ($89/mo, $71/mo yearly)

GPT-5 is the most advanced AI model available in Rypl. It includes all the capabilities of GPT-4.1 plus full conversational memory, supportive replies, and the highest quality response generation.

### Strengths

- **Full conversational memory:** Remembers previous interactions with the same commenter across sessions
- **Supportive replies:** Goes beyond answering questions to proactively support the customer's journey
- **Highest reply quality:** Most natural, nuanced, and contextually appropriate replies
- **Complex query handling:** Handles multi-faceted questions, comparisons, and edge cases with ease
- **Advanced analytics:** Deeper insights into comment patterns and engagement metrics
- **Multi-turn mastery:** Manages extended conversations naturally, remembering context from earlier exchanges

### Best For

- High-volume advertisers where reply quality has significant revenue impact
- Agencies managing large client portfolios (unlimited accounts and pages)
- Businesses with complex products that require detailed, nuanced explanations
- Brands where customer experience and communication quality are top priorities
- Companies that want the most advanced AI capabilities available

### Key Advantage: Conversational Memory

On the Ultra plan, your AI agent maintains full conversational memory. This means:

- If a customer comments on Monday asking about a product and returns on Wednesday, the agent remembers the previous conversation
- Follow-up questions are answered in context ("I mentioned it comes in blue — would you like me to send the link?")
- Returning customers get a personalized experience
- Multi-turn conversations feel natural and coherent

### Key Advantage: Supportive Replies

Supportive replies go beyond answering questions. GPT-5 can:

- Proactively suggest related products based on the customer's expressed needs
- Offer reassurance on common concerns before the customer asks
- Provide buying guidance that feels genuinely helpful rather than pushy
- Anticipate follow-up questions and address them preemptively

## Choosing the Right Model

### Decision Framework

Ask yourself these questions:

1. **Do I need knowledge base integration?**
   - No → Simple (GPT-4 mini) works well
   - Yes → Pro (GPT-4.1) or Ultra (GPT-5)

2. **Do I need intent detection?**
   - No → Simple is sufficient
   - Yes → Pro or Ultra

3. **Do I need conversational memory?**
   - No → Simple or Pro
   - Yes → Ultra (GPT-5)

4. **How important is reply quality to my revenue?**
   - Basic is fine → Simple
   - Important → Pro
   - Critical → Ultra

### Upgrade Path

You can upgrade your plan at any time from [app.rypl.is](https://app.rypl.is). The transition is seamless — your agent configurations, knowledge base, and settings carry over. The new AI model takes effect immediately.
