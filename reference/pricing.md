## Plan Recommendations

### Simple ($19/mo / $15/mo yearly)

**Best for:** Solo entrepreneurs and small businesses managing a single Facebook Page with moderate comment volume.

Choose Simple if you:
- Run ads on 1-3 Facebook Pages
- Need basic spam moderation and auto-replies
- Want to get started with AI comment management at the lowest cost
- Have straightforward products or services that do not require extensive knowledge base support

### Pro ($49/mo / $39/mo yearly)

**Best for:** Growing businesses and small agencies running multiple ad campaigns who need advanced AI capabilities.

Choose Pro if you:
- Manage up to 5 Facebook accounts and 20 Pages
- Want context-aware AI replies powered by GPT-4.1
- Need intent detection to understand customer behavior
- Want to upload a knowledge base for accurate, detailed responses
- Need advanced moderation rules and analytics
- Run campaigns in multiple languages or industries

This is the **most popular plan** for businesses serious about maximizing their Facebook ad ROI.

### Ultra ($89/mo / $71/mo yearly)

**Best for:** Agencies, high-volume advertisers, and businesses that need the most powerful AI capabilities.

Choose Ultra if you:
- Manage unlimited Facebook accounts and Pages
- Need the most advanced AI model (GPT-5) for the highest quality replies
- Want full conversational memory for multi-turn interactions
- Require priority support and fast queue processing
- Need advanced analytics for detailed performance insights
- Are an agency managing many clients from one dashboard

## Billing Details

### Payment Processing

All billing is handled securely through **Lemon Squeezy**. You can manage your subscription, update payment information, and download invoices at any time.

### Yearly Savings

Yearly billing saves you approximately 20% compared to monthly billing:

| Plan | Monthly | Yearly (per month) | Annual Savings |
|------|---------|-------------------|----------------|
| Simple | $19 | $15 | $48/year |
| Pro | $49 | $39 | $120/year |
| Ultra | $89 | $71 | $216/year |

### 7-Day Free Trial

- Available on all plans
- Full access to all features on your chosen plan tier
- No hidden limitations during the trial period
- Cancel before the trial ends and you will not be charged
- Upgrade or downgrade at any time during the trial

### Cancellation

- Cancel at any time from your Lemon Squeezy billing portal
- Access continues until the end of your current billing cycle
- Settings and configuration are saved — if you reactivate later, everything is still there
- No cancellation fees or penalties

### Changing Plans

- Upgrade or downgrade at any time
- Changes take effect immediately
- Billing is prorated when switching between plans
