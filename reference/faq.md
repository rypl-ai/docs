## General

### How does RYPL turn comments into sales?

Rypl uses AI to automatically reply to Facebook ad comments in real time. When someone asks about your product in a comment, Rypl detects the intent, pulls relevant information from your knowledge base, and posts a helpful, on-brand reply — all in under 60 seconds.

The AI performs sentiment analysis to understand what the commenter needs, whether it is pricing, product details, shipping information, or support. By responding instantly with accurate, relevant information, Rypl converts casual interest into purchase decisions. On average, businesses see approximately a 10% increase in sales from their Facebook ads within the first month, with 28% more comment conversations converting to sales.

Rypl works 24/7, so every comment gets a response — even at nights, on weekends, and during holidays.

### Does RYPL support multiple languages?

Yes, Rypl supports all languages worldwide. The AI automatically detects the language of each incoming comment and responds in that same language. There is no need to configure language settings or create separate agents for different languages.

Whether your audience comments in English, Spanish, French, Arabic, Japanese, Portuguese, or any other language, Rypl handles it seamlessly. This is particularly valuable for businesses running international ad campaigns.

### Does RYPL work for multiple Pages?

Yes, Rypl supports multi-Page management from a single dashboard. This is especially useful for marketing agencies managing Facebook ads for multiple clients.

You can customize AI agents for each client with unique brand voices, knowledge bases, and moderation rules. The number of Pages you can manage depends on your plan: Simple supports 3 Pages, Pro supports 20, and Ultra offers unlimited Pages.

### What's the deal with Rypl's pricing?

Rypl does not offer a free plan. Every subscription starts with a **7-day free trial** that gives you full access to all features on your chosen plan tier.

Plans start at $19/month for the Simple plan, $49/month for Pro, and $89/month for Ultra. Yearly billing gives you a discount: $15/month, $39/month, and $71/month respectively.

You can cancel anytime, and you will retain access until the end of your billing cycle. See the full [Pricing page](pricing.md) for detailed plan comparisons.

### Does RYPL work for e-commerce and B2B?

Yes, Rypl works for both e-commerce and B2B businesses. The platform is flexible enough to handle any vertical.

For **e-commerce**, you can upload product catalogs, configure pricing responses, and handle "How much?" and "Is this available?" questions automatically.

For **B2B**, you can set a professional tone, provide detailed service information, and route qualified leads to your sales team. You can tailor the AI's tone, prompts, and product information for each specific use case.

See our detailed guides for [E-Commerce](../use-cases/ecommerce.md), [Agencies](../use-cases/agencies.md), and [B2B Services](../use-cases/b2b-services.md).

## Performance

### How fast is Rypl AI?

Rypl operates at two speeds:

- **Moderation:** Spam and negative comments are detected and hidden in approximately **1.7 seconds**. This is fast enough that most of your audience will never see the spam.
- **AI Replies:** Context-aware responses are generated and posted in **under 60 seconds**. This includes analyzing the comment, detecting intent, searching the knowledge base, generating the reply in your brand voice, and posting it.

Based on user data, businesses using Rypl typically see:

- Approximately **10% increase in sales** from their Facebook ads within the first month

- **28% more conversations** converted into sales through timely, relevant AI replies

These results come from the combination of instant replies (capturing interest before it fades), clean comment sections (maintaining social proof), and intent-driven response strategies.

## Platform

### Does Rypl work with Instagram?

Currently, Rypl is focused on **Facebook only**. Instagram support is on the product roadmap for a future release.

If you need Instagram comment management, check the [blog](https://blog.rypl.is) for updates on Instagram integration.

### Is Rypl safe to use? Does it comply with Meta's policies?

Yes, Rypl is fully safe and compliant. The platform is built entirely on **Meta's Official Graph API**, which is the sanctioned API framework for interacting with Facebook Pages programmatically.

This means:
- **Zero ban risk** — Your Page will never be flagged or restricted for using Rypl
- **Full compliance** — Rypl operates within Meta's Terms of Service
- **Secure connections** — All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
- **Token-based access** — Rypl stores secure API tokens, never your Facebook password

## Getting Started

### How do I set up AI auto-replies for my Facebook ads?

Setting up Rypl takes just a few minutes:

1. **Sign up** at [app.rypl.is/signup](https://app.rypl.is/signup) and start your 7-day free trial
2. **Connect your Facebook Page** through Meta's secure OAuth flow
3. **Create an AI agent** with your brand name and purpose
4. **Configure your brand voice** — set the tone, style, and example replies
5. **Upload your knowledge base** — product catalog (PDF), FAQ (CSV), or website URLs
6. **Activate** your agent and start receiving automated, on-brand replies

For a detailed walkthrough, see the [Getting Started guide](../getting-started/).
