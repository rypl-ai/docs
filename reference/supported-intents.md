# Supported Intents

Rypl's intent detection system classifies every incoming Facebook ad comment into one of eight categories. This classification happens in real time and is used to drive smarter AI replies, prioritize high-value interactions, and provide actionable analytics.

Available on **Pro** and **Ultra** plans.

---

## Intent Overview

| # | Intent | Color | Signal |
|---|--------|-------|--------|
| 1 | Purchase | Green | Ready-to-buy signals |
| 2 | Price Inquiry | Yellow | Cost, discounts, availability |
| 3 | Return Request | Red | Refunds, exchanges |
| 4 | Positive Feedback | Teal | Compliments, testimonials |
| 5 | Complaint | Orange | Negative experiences |
| 6 | Support | Blue | Help, technical questions |
| 7 | Product Info | Purple | Specs, features, compatibility |
| 8 | Shipping | Cyan | Delivery, tracking, costs |

---

## Detailed Intent Reference

### 1. Purchase (Green)

**Description:** The commenter is expressing a clear intent to buy, order, or acquire the product or service being advertised. These are your highest-value comments.

**Example comments:**
- "I want to buy this! How do I order?"
- "Take my money!"
- "Can I get two of these?"
- "Is this still available? I need it"
- "How do I place an order?"
- "Adding this to my cart right now"
- "Shut up and take my money"
- "I need this in my life"

**Recommended response strategy:**
- Prioritize speed — respond as quickly as possible while interest is high
- Include a direct link to purchase
- Mention any active discounts or promotional codes
- Make the buying process as simple as possible
- If multiple variants exist, ask which one they prefer to keep the conversation moving
- Thank them for their interest

**Analytics insight:** High Purchase intent across your ads indicates strong product-market fit and effective ad creative. Invest more in these campaigns.

---

### 2. Price Inquiry (Yellow)

**Description:** The commenter is asking about cost, looking for discounts, inquiring about payment options, or checking if the product is within their budget.

**Example comments:**
- "How much is this?"
- "What's the price?"
- "Do you have any discount codes?"
- "Is this on sale?"
- "How much with shipping included?"
- "Any payment plans available?"
- "Is there a student discount?"
- "What's the price for the large size?"

**Recommended response strategy:**
- Provide the exact price from your knowledge base
- Mention any active promotions, bundle deals, or discount codes
- Include a direct link to the product page
- If the price is higher than competitors, emphasize value (quality, warranty, free shipping)
- If you offer payment plans or financing, mention it

**Analytics insight:** High Price Inquiry volume suggests strong interest but potential price sensitivity. Consider A/B testing promotions or highlighting value propositions in your ad creative.

---

### 3. Return Request (Red)

**Description:** The commenter is asking about refunds, returns, exchanges, or expressing dissatisfaction with a purchase.

**Example comments:**
- "I want to return this, it doesn't fit"
- "How do I get a refund?"
- "Can I exchange this for a different size?"
- "This isn't what I expected, can I send it back?"
- "What's your return policy?"
- "I want my money back"
- "The product arrived damaged"

**Recommended response strategy:**
- Respond empathetically — acknowledge their frustration or concern
- Provide your return/exchange policy clearly
- Direct them to DMs for personal assistance with their specific order
- If the issue involves damage, offer a replacement or immediate resolution
- Flag for human review if the situation is complex or high-value

**Analytics insight:** Frequent Return Request intents may indicate issues with product quality, sizing guidance, or customer expectations set by ad creative.

---

### 4. Positive Feedback (Teal)

**Description:** The commenter is leaving a compliment, sharing a positive experience, or expressing satisfaction with the product or service.

**Example comments:**
- "Love this product! Best purchase ever!"
- "Your customer service is amazing"
- "Just got mine and it's even better than expected!"
- "Highly recommend this to everyone"
- "Five stars! Would buy again"
- "This changed my life"
- "Best brand I've ever bought from"

**Recommended response strategy:**
- Thank them warmly and sincerely
- Encourage them to share their experience with friends (tag, share)
- Ask if they would be willing to leave a review on your website
- Acknowledge specific compliments ("We're glad you love the quality!")
- Use the interaction to boost visibility (positive comment replies get more engagement)

**Analytics insight:** High Positive Feedback volume is a strong indicator of customer satisfaction and can be highlighted in future ad creative as social proof.

---

### 5. Complaint (Orange)

**Description:** The commenter is expressing dissatisfaction, frustration, or sharing a negative experience with the product, service, or brand.

**Example comments:**
- "Still waiting for my order, it's been 3 weeks"
- "The quality is terrible"
- "Worst customer service ever"
- "This is nothing like the pictures"
- "Terrible experience, would not recommend"
- "I've been trying to reach support for days"
- "Complete waste of money"

**Recommended response strategy:**
- Respond empathetically — do not be defensive
- Acknowledge their frustration specifically
- Offer a concrete solution or next step
- Move the conversation to DMs to resolve the issue privately
- Flag for human follow-up if the complaint is severe
- Never argue or dismiss concerns publicly

**Analytics insight:** Monitor Complaint patterns by ad campaign. If specific ads generate more complaints, review the ad creative for misleading claims or unmet expectations.

---

### 6. Support (Blue)

**Description:** The commenter needs help, has a technical question, or requires assistance with the product or their account.

**Example comments:**
- "How do I track my order?"
- "The app isn't working for me"
- "Can someone help me with my account?"
- "I need help choosing the right size"
- "How do I use this feature?"
- "My login isn't working"
- "Can you help me set this up?"

**Recommended response strategy:**
- Provide helpful, accurate information from the knowledge base
- Link to relevant documentation, FAQ pages, or support resources
- Offer to continue the conversation in DMs for detailed troubleshooting
- If the issue requires technical support, direct them to the appropriate channel
- Be patient and thorough in explanations

**Analytics insight:** Common Support questions should be addressed in your FAQ and knowledge base to improve AI reply quality.

---

### 7. Product Info (Purple)

**Description:** The commenter is asking about product specifications, features, compatibility, materials, dimensions, or other factual details.

**Example comments:**
- "What material is this made of?"
- "Does this work with iPhone 15?"
- "What are the dimensions?"
- "Is this waterproof?"
- "How many does this come with?"
- "What's the battery life?"
- "Is this compatible with my existing setup?"

**Recommended response strategy:**
- Pull accurate specifications from your knowledge base
- Be specific and detailed — vague answers do not satisfy Product Info inquiries
- Link to the full product page for comprehensive details
- If the information is not available in the knowledge base, acknowledge that honestly and offer to find out
- Use the opportunity to highlight standout features

**Analytics insight:** High Product Info volume may indicate your ad creative needs more detailed product information, or that your audience is in the research phase.

---

### 8. Shipping (Cyan)

**Description:** The commenter is asking about delivery times, shipping costs, tracking, international shipping, or logistics.

**Example comments:**
- "How long does shipping take?"
- "Do you ship internationally?"
- "Where's my order?"
- "How much is shipping to Australia?"
- "Do you offer free shipping?"
- "Can I get expedited delivery?"
- "What carrier do you use?"

**Recommended response strategy:**
- Provide clear shipping details from your knowledge base
- Include delivery timeframes for their specific region if possible
- Mention free shipping thresholds ("Free shipping on orders over $50!")
- For tracking questions, direct them to DMs with their order number
- If you do not ship to their location, be upfront about it

**Analytics insight:** High Shipping intent across campaigns targeting specific regions may indicate demand in those markets. Consider adjusting shipping policies or highlighting delivery benefits in ad creative.

---

## Using Intent Data Strategically

Beyond individual replies, intent data provides strategic insights:

1. **Ad creative optimization** — Which ads generate the most Purchase intents? Invest more in those.
2. **Knowledge base gaps** — Which intents produce the weakest AI replies? Add more information for those topics.
3. **Campaign targeting** — Different audiences may have different intent distributions. Use this to refine targeting.
4. **Product development** — Repeated questions about missing features or options inform your product roadmap.
