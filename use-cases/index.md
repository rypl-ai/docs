# Use Cases

[Rypl](https://www.rypl.is) serves a wide range of businesses that advertise on Facebook. While the core platform is the same, the way you configure and leverage Rypl varies depending on your business model, audience, and goals.

This section provides detailed guides for the most common business types using Rypl.

---

## Industry Guides

### [E-Commerce](ecommerce.md)

Online stores and direct-to-consumer brands that sell physical or digital products through Facebook ads. This guide covers product catalog integration, handling pricing questions, managing availability queries, dealing with size/color/variant questions, and optimizing for purchase conversions.

**Key benefits for e-commerce:**
- AI replies to "How much?" and "Does this come in...?" questions drive immediate sales
- Fast moderation keeps ad comments clean, protecting social proof
- Intent detection identifies purchase-ready customers for prioritized responses
- Knowledge base integration with product catalogs enables accurate, detailed replies

### [Marketing Agencies](agencies.md)

Agencies managing Facebook ads for multiple clients. This guide covers multi-client management, scalable agent setup, per-client brand voice and knowledge base configuration, client reporting, and team workflow optimization.

**Key benefits for agencies:**
- One dashboard for all clients — no switching between tools
- Per-client AI agents with unique voices and knowledge bases
- Scalable setup: onboard a new client in 10-15 minutes
- Client-facing analytics and performance reports
- Pro and Ultra plans support 5+ accounts and 20+ pages

### [B2B Services](b2b-services.md)

Service-based businesses using Facebook ads to generate leads, book appointments, and handle service inquiries. This guide covers appointment booking flows, qualification of service inquiries, support ticket routing, and professional communication standards.

**Key benefits for B2B:**
- Professional, knowledgeable AI replies that match B2B communication standards
- Intent detection identifies high-quality leads (Purchase, Price Inquiry intents)
- Support questions are handled or routed appropriately
- 24/7 availability means no lead goes unanswered, even outside business hours

|---------------|-------------|-------------------------|-----------------|

| Agency | Manage multiple clients efficiently | Multi-account, per-client agents | Pro or Ultra |
| B2B Services | Generate and qualify leads | Professional voice, service info | Simple or Pro |

## Common Across All Use Cases

Regardless of your business type, these fundamentals apply:

1. **Upload your FAQ** — This is the single most impactful knowledge base addition for any business
2. **Set a specific brand voice** — Every business benefits from clear, detailed brand voice guidelines
3. **Monitor for the first week** — Review AI replies daily during setup to catch and correct issues early
4. **Use intent data** — Track which intents are most common to understand your audience better
5. **Keep knowledge base current** — Outdated information leads to inaccurate replies and lost trust
