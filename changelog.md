## v1.0.0 — March 2026

**Initial Release**

Rypl launches as a purpose-built AI platform for Facebook ad comment management. This release includes the full suite of features designed to help businesses turn ad engagement into revenue.

### Features

- **AI Comments & Messages** — Context-aware auto-replies to ad comments and DMs powered by GPT-4 mini, GPT-4.1, and GPT-5 depending on plan tier.
- **Fast Moderation** — Spam and negative comments detected and hidden in approximately 1.7 seconds.
- **Intent Detection** — Eight intent categories (Purchase, Price Inquiry, Return Request, Positive Feedback, Complaint, Support, Product Info, Shipping) automatically classified for every incoming comment.
- **Knowledge Base** — Upload product catalogs (PDF), FAQ answers (CSV), brand voice guides (TXT), website URLs, databases, and leverage user comments and conversation history as sources.
- **Multi-Account Support** — Connect multiple Facebook Pages with per-client AI agent customization. Plans support 3, 20, or unlimited pages.
- **Smart Tracking** — Conversion data, response rates, and engagement metrics tracked in a unified dashboard for ROAS optimization.
- **Multi-Language** — Automatic language detection and response in any language worldwide.
- **Meta Official API** — Built entirely on Meta's Official Graph API for full compliance and zero ban risk.

### Plans

- **Simple** — $19/mo ($15/mo yearly) with GPT-4 mini
- **Pro** — $49/mo ($39/mo yearly) with GPT-4.1
- **Ultra** — $89/mo ($71/mo yearly) with GPT-5

All plans include a 7-day free trial.
