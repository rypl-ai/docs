# Troubleshooting

Running into an issue with [Rypl](https://www.rypl.is)? This section covers the most common problems and their solutions. If you cannot find the answer here, contact our team at [hey@rypl.is](mailto:hey@rypl.is).

---

## Common Issues

### [Facebook Connection Issues](facebook-connection.md)

Problems connecting your Facebook Page to Rypl, including OAuth errors, permission issues, expired tokens, and reconnection procedures. Start here if Rypl cannot access your Facebook Page.

**Common symptoms:**
- "Connection failed" error during setup
- Facebook permission request not appearing
- Page not showing in the list of available Pages
- Previously connected Page no longer working

### [Agent Not Replying](agent-not-replying.md)

Your AI agent is connected but is not posting replies to comments. This guide walks through a complete diagnostic checklist to identify and fix the issue.

**Common symptoms:**
- Agent shows as active but comments go unanswered
- Some comments get replies but others do not
- Replies are slow or delayed
- Agent was working before but stopped

### [Billing Issues](billing.md)

Questions and problems related to billing, plan changes, cancellation, and refunds. All billing is managed through Lemon Squeezy.

**Common symptoms:**
- Unable to change plan
- Payment method needs updating
- Questions about cancellation or refunds
- Trial period questions

---

## Quick Diagnostic Steps

Before diving into specific troubleshooting guides, run through this quick checklist:

1. **Is your agent active?** — Check the agent status in your Rypl dashboard. It should show as "Active."
2. **Is your Facebook Page connected?** — Verify the connection status in your account settings. Look for a green/active indicator.
3. **Are you on the right plan?** — Some features (knowledge base, intent detection) require Pro or Ultra plans.
4. **Has your trial expired?** — If your 7-day trial ended without selecting a paid plan, your agent may be paused.
5. **Check the dashboard for errors** — Rypl logs errors and warnings. Look for any red flags or error messages.

If these basic checks do not reveal the issue, proceed to the relevant troubleshooting guide above.

## Getting Help

If you cannot resolve your issue with these guides:

- **Email:** [hey@rypl.is](mailto:hey@rypl.is) — We aim to respond within 24 hours
- **Priority support:** Available on the Ultra plan for faster response times
- **Blog:** [blog.rypl.is](https://blog.rypl.is) — Check for announcements about known issues or maintenance

When contacting support, include:

- Your Rypl account email
- The Facebook Page(s) affected
- A description of the issue and when it started
- Any error messages you are seeing
- Steps you have already tried
