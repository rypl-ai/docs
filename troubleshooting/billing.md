# Billing Troubleshooting

All [Rypl](https://www.rypl.is) billing is managed through **Lemon Squeezy**, a secure payment platform. This guide covers common billing questions and how to resolve them.

---

## Managing Your Subscription

### How to Access Billing Settings

1. Log in to your Rypl account at [app.rypl.is](https://app.rypl.is)
2. Navigate to your account settings or billing section
3. Click the link to manage your subscription — this will open the Lemon Squeezy customer portal
4. From there, you can update payment methods, change plans, download invoices, and manage your subscription

### Updating Payment Information

If your credit card has expired or you need to change your payment method:

1. Access the Lemon Squeezy billing portal through your Rypl account
2. Navigate to Payment Methods
3. Add your new payment method
4. Remove the old one if desired
5. Your next billing cycle will use the updated payment method

If a payment has already failed due to an expired card, updating your payment method should trigger a retry of the failed payment.

## Plan Changes

### Upgrading Your Plan

You can upgrade from Simple to Pro, or from Pro to Ultra, at any time:

1. Go to your Rypl account settings
2. Select the plan you want to upgrade to
3. Confirm the upgrade
4. The new features are available immediately
5. Billing is prorated — you only pay the difference for the remainder of your current billing cycle

### Downgrading Your Plan

You can downgrade at any time:

1. Go to your account settings
2. Select the lower-tier plan
3. Confirm the downgrade
4. The change takes effect at the start of your next billing cycle
5. You retain access to your current plan's features until then

**Important:** When downgrading, be aware of plan limits:

| Downgrade | What to Check |
|-----------|---------------|
| Ultra to Pro | You must have 5 or fewer accounts and 20 or fewer Pages |
| Pro to Simple | You must have 1 account, 3 or fewer Pages, and 2 or fewer agents |
| Any downgrade | Knowledge base and intent detection features are only available on Pro+ |

If you exceed the lower plan's limits, you will need to reduce your connected accounts, Pages, or agents before the downgrade takes effect.

### Switching Between Monthly and Yearly

You can switch between monthly and yearly billing:

- **Monthly to Yearly:** Saves approximately 20%. The change takes effect immediately.
- **Yearly to Monthly:** Takes effect at the end of your current yearly term.

Yearly savings by plan:

| Plan | Monthly | Yearly (per month) | You Save |
|------|---------|-------------------|----------|
| Simple | $19 | $15 | $48/year |
| Pro | $49 | $39 | $120/year |
| Ultra | $89 | $71 | $216/year |

## 7-Day Free Trial

### How the Trial Works

- Every plan starts with a 7-day free trial
- Full access to all features on your chosen plan tier
- You will not be charged until the trial period ends
- Cancel before the trial ends to avoid charges

### Trial Expiration

If your trial expires and you have not selected a paid plan:

- Your AI agent will be paused (it stops processing comments)
- Your configuration, knowledge base, and settings are preserved
- Subscribe to any paid plan to reactivate immediately

### Extending a Trial

Contact [hey@rypl.is](mailto:hey@rypl.is) if you need additional time to evaluate Rypl. We are happy to discuss options on a case-by-case basis.

## Cancellation

### How to Cancel

1. Access the Lemon Squeezy billing portal through your Rypl account
2. Select "Cancel Subscription"
3. Confirm the cancellation

### What Happens After Cancellation

- You retain full access until the end of your current billing cycle
- Your AI agents stop processing comments after the billing cycle ends
- Your account, configurations, knowledge base, and settings are saved
- If you reactivate in the future, everything is preserved — you can resume right where you left off
- No cancellation fees or penalties

### Reactivating After Cancellation

To reactivate:

1. Log in to [app.rypl.is](https://app.rypl.is)
2. Choose a plan and subscribe
3. Your previous configurations are restored automatically
4. Agents can be reactivated immediately

## Refunds

### Refund Policy

Rypl handles refund requests on a case-by-case basis. If you are unhappy with the service:

1. Contact [hey@rypl.is](mailto:hey@rypl.is) with your account email and reason for the refund request
2. Include the billing period in question
3. Our team will review and respond within 48 hours

### Common Refund Scenarios

- **Accidental charge after trial:** If you forgot to cancel before the trial ended, contact us promptly for resolution
- **Service issues:** If Rypl was not functioning correctly during the billing period, provide details about the issue
- **Double billing:** If you were charged twice, contact us with transaction details for immediate resolution

## Payment Issues

### Failed Payment

If a payment fails:

1. Check that your payment method is current and has sufficient funds
2. Update your payment information in the Lemon Squeezy portal
3. Lemon Squeezy will automatically retry the payment
4. If the retry succeeds, your service continues uninterrupted
5. If it fails again, your account may be paused until the payment is resolved

### Invoice Requests

Download invoices from the Lemon Squeezy billing portal. All past invoices are available for download in PDF format.

### Currency

Rypl prices are listed in USD. Lemon Squeezy handles currency conversion automatically based on your payment method.

## Common Questions

### Can I pause my subscription instead of cancelling?

Currently, Rypl does not offer a pause option. If you need to temporarily stop using the service, you can cancel and reactivate later. Your settings will be preserved.

### Do I get charged for the trial?

No. The 7-day trial is completely free. You are only charged when the trial period ends and you continue with a paid plan.

### What payment methods are accepted?

Lemon Squeezy accepts major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and other payment methods available in your region.
